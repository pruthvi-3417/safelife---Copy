💡 How It Works
The 4-7-8 technique involves inhaling for 4 seconds, holding for 7 seconds, and exhaling for 8 seconds. This pattern activates your parasympathetic nervous system, reducing anxiety and helping you fall asleep faster.
😴 Reduces insomnia by calming racing thoughts
💓 Lowers heart rate and blood pressure
🧠 Reduces cortisol (stress hormone) levels
🎯 Improves focus and mental clarity
